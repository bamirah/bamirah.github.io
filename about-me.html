Amirah Brown

Hi! Many of you who have arrived at this page probably already know me by now so consider this page a formality.

My name is Amirah Brown. I was born and raised in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. I attended Milwaukee Public Schools my entire K12 career. After I graduated from Rufus King High School, I attended the University of Wisconsin-Madison, where I received my Bachelor of Science in Elementary Education with a concentration in math teaching. I accepted my first teaching role out of undergrad, teaching 6th/7th-grade math in West Philly. It was an overwhelming experience, leaving me with a lot of anxiety and sadness about classroom teaching. I left teaching after my first year and decided to pursue a master's degree. I accepted an offer from the University of Michigan-Ann Arbor, where I will soon graduate with my master's in Educational Studies. I hope to re-enter the field of teaching with a new sense of self and stability.

Teaching is hard. Give yourself grace. I wish I would have.

Love you many!

Amirah B.
